Key features on all our Payroll software

Create multiple companies

With just one Quick Payroll software package, you can run the accounts for multiple companies, so long as each is represented by at least one employee. This solution even supports multiple company files.

Integration by means of journal

When you’re using one of our Payroll packages, journal integration with both QuickBooks Accountant, Pro and Premier is automatic. This means you’ll be able to draw on all of your company’s accounting data when calculating salaries.

Weekly, fortnightly & monthly pay runs

No matter how many different pay schedules you have to run, Quick Payroll software will simply and conveniently keep track of all outgoing finances. With us, your employees are sure to get paid on time.

Access control

Regardless of the size of your accounts department, you can enjoy tight security through password protection and user-set permission levels. This guarantees that your data can only be read by authorised parties.

Reports, summaries and UIF submissions

Our software allows you to produce and manage huge amounts of data, from performance details and records to staff contact information. This ensures that you remain organised and effective in your business administration.

E@syfile integration EMP501 submissions

We bring you superior workflow integration in the E@syfile format. Additionally, our software takes the stress out of your compulsory EMP501 submissions, by facilitating hassle-free record and document storage.
